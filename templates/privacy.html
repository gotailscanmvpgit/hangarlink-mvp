Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 4, 2026

1 Introduction

Welcome to HangarLinks.com (referred to as "HangarLinks," "we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, share, and protect information when you use our website, mobile application, and related services (collectively, the "Services").

By access or using our Services, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our Services.

2 Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide:

  • Account Data: Name, email address, phone number, and password when you register.
  • Profile and Listing Data: Aircraft tail numbers, hangar dimensions, airport ICAO codes, photos, and descriptions you upload.
  • Payment Information: We use Stripe to process payments. We do not store your full credit card details on our servers; Stripe provides us with tokens and transaction status.
  • Communication: Messages sent between users via our internal messaging system and inquiries sent to our AI Concierge.

Information Collected Automatically:

  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and pages visited.
  • Location Data: Based on your IP address or GPS (with permission), to provide relevant local hangar availability and fuel prices.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to maintain your session and remember your preferences.

Third-Party Data:

  • We receive information from partners such as AviationStack (for airport and FBO data) and Stripe (to confirm subscription status).

3 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for several purposes, including:

  • To Provide Services: Facilitating the search, listing, and booking of aircraft parking spaces.
  • To Improve the App: Analyzing usage patterns to enhance UI/UX and training our AI Concierge to provide more accurate aviation-specific assistance.
  • To Personalize Experience: Recommending hangars or FBO services based on your aircraft type and frequent airports.
  • To Communicate: Sending booking confirmations, security alerts, market intelligence updates, and marketing communications (which you can opt-out of).
  • For Safety and Compliance: Verifying user identities and ensuring compliance with FAA, Transport Canada (CARs), and local airport regulations.

4 How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We share your information only in the following contexts:

  • Between Users: When a booking is initiated, relevant contact and aircraft information is shared between the owner and renter to facilitate the transaction.
  • Service Providers: We share data with Stripe (payments), SendGrid (email), and our AI service providers (LLM processing) to operate the platform.
  • Partners: If you opt-in to micro-insurance or FBO services, we may share basic details with those third-party providers to generate quotes.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, such as a subpoena or government request, particularly regarding FAA/CARs compliance audits.

5 Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights under the GDPR, CCPA, or PIPEDA, including:

  • Access and Portability: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can update your profile information at any time via your user dashboard.
  • Deletion: You can request that we delete your account and personal data, subject to certain legal obligations (like tax and transaction records).
  • Opt-Out: You can unsubscribe from marketing emails via the "unsubscribe" link or by contacting support.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@hangarlinks.com.

6 Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including SSL/TLS encryption for all data in transit and AES encryption for data at rest. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7 Children's Privacy

HangarLinks is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete it.

8 Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
